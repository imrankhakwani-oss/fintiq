## DAY 10
**Article:** What is the Efficient Frontier? Modern Portfolio Theory Explained

---

Harry Markowitz won the Nobel Prize for this idea.

Two portfolios can have the same return — but very different risk. The better portfolio gives you the same return for less risk.

The Efficient Frontier is the curve of all the portfolios that do this optimally. Anything below the curve is suboptimal. You're taking more risk than necessary.

Most retail investors are sitting way below the frontier without knowing it.

## DAY 12
**Article:** Cointegration Explained: The Strategy Institutional Traders Use

---

Shell and BP both drill oil. Both are exposed to the same oil price, the same North Sea regulations, the same refining margins.

When they drift apart — Shell up 8%, BP flat — professional traders ask: which one is wrong?

Usually both revert to each other. That's cointegration. And it's the statistical basis for one of the most consistent strategies used by hedge funds.

You don't need to be short-selling from your ISA to benefit from understanding this. The same logic applies to any correlated pair.
