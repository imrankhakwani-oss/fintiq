## ABOUT SECTION
*(Your story — 2,600 characters max. This is the most important section.)*

Most retail investors lose money not because markets are against them — but because they never had a method.

I've spent 16 years in finance — starting at KPMG and EY, then advising 100+ businesses and high-net-worth clients as a Senior Accountant and CFO. I've seen what separates investors who build wealth from those who speculate and lose. The difference is almost never intelligence or information. It's process.

Retail investors are flooded with tips, hot takes, and surface-level research. They buy because they think a price will go up. They sell because they're losing money. They never ask: why am I buying? What does the data say? What's the probability of success? What would a professional fund manager do?

That's why I built **Fintiq**.

Fintiq gives everyday investors the same analytical tools used by professional trading desks and institutional fund managers — without requiring a Bloomberg terminal, a finance degree, or years of experience.

→ **Multi-factor stock screener** — screen the FTSE 100, NASDAQ, NYSE and global markets on quality, value and momentum simultaneously
→ **Monte Carlo simulation** — 10,000 scenarios to show the realistic range of outcomes, not a single misleading forecast
→ **Portfolio optimisation using Modern Portfolio Theory** — mathematically optimal allocation using Nobel Prize-winning Markowitz framework
→ **Statistical pairs trading** — market-neutral strategies that professional hedge funds use to profit in any market condition
→ **20 free educational guides** — from DCF valuation to FIRE retirement planning, written specifically for UK investors

The mission is simple: help retail investors stop speculating and start investing like professionals.
